Mauritius information:

Mauritius, nestled in the Indian Ocean, boasts a mesmerizing coastline adorned with pristine beaches, coral reefs, and azure lagoons. The diverse coastal landscape ranges from the dramatic cliffs of Gris-Gris to the powder-soft sands of Flic-en-Flac, offering a kaleidoscope of experiences for visitors. With its warm, inviting waters and vibrant marine life, Mauritius' coast is not only a paradise for beach lovers but also a haven for snorkelers and water adventurers.
