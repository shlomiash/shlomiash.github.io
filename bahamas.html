Bahamas information:

The Bahamas boasts a stunning coastline that spans over 700 islands and 2,400 cays, offering a diverse and breathtaking seascape. With crystal-clear turquoise waters and pristine white sandy beaches, the coast of the Bahamas is a tropical paradise that attracts visitors from around the world. The vibrant coral reefs along the coast also make it a haven for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts, providing a rich underwater ecosystem to explore.
